How to Play

Objective

Select 5 Premier League players to maximise your score for the category shown at the top (e.g. Goals, Assists, Clean Sheets).

How to Play

Submit one player per row. Each row has up to three requirements:

  • Club(s) — the player must have appeared for that club in the Premier League.
  • Season range — the appearance must fall within those years.
  • Qualifier — an optional extra condition (e.g. "WON PL TITLE").

Qualifiers are tagged SAME SEASON (must be met in the submitted season) or CAREER (met at any point in their PL career).

Scoring

When you submit a valid player, their best matching season's stat is added to your total score.

Example: submitting a player who scored 32 goals in their best valid season adds 32 to your score.

Percentile

The percentile beneath each stat shows how your answer ranks against every possible valid answer for that row. 100% = the best possible pick.

Easy Mode

Easy Mode is always on — just submit a player's name and the best valid season is automatically chosen for you.
