How to Play

Objective

Fill all 5 rows by picking Premier League players that match each row's constraints. Try to maximise your total score — or toggle TARGET mode and aim to hit a specific number instead.

Each Row Has Three Constraints

  • Club(s) — the player must have appeared for that club in the Premier League. No club shown means any club counts.
  • Season range — the player must have appeared within those years.
  • Qualifier — an optional extra condition such as nationality, position, or award.

Qualifiers tagged SAME SEASON must be met in the season they played; qualifiers tagged CAREER must be met at any point in their PL career.

Scoring

Each puzzle has a category (e.g. Appearances, Goals) and a mode:

  • Season — your score for that row is the player's best single-season total within the constraints.
  • Career — your score is the sum of that stat across every valid season.

Target Mode

Press TARGET in the footer to switch modes. Instead of maximising, try to get your total score as close as possible to the target number. The score bar shows how much you have remaining — or how far over you've gone.

Percentile Tiers

Each result is colour-coded based on how your answer ranks against all valid answers for that row:

  • ■ Purple — 100th percentile (the single best answer)
  • ■ Blue — 95th–99th percentile
  • ■ Gold — 80th–94th percentile
  • ■ Silver — 70th–79th percentile
  • ■ Bronze — 60th–69th percentile

Tips

  • You don't need to type accents — "Ruben" will find Rúben Dias.
  • The best valid season or career total is automatically selected for you.
  • Use Give Up on a row if you're stuck — it won't count as a guess.
  • Hit SHARE after finishing to copy your results and send to friends.
